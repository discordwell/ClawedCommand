The Clawed faction style — scrappy mouse resistance. Small-scale, angular, triangular shapes with pointed roofs and sharp corners. Earthy warm browns (#8D6E63 primary, #A1887F secondary) with bold green accents (#4CAF50). Rough-hewn wood, packed earth walls, patched burlap cloth, hand-forged iron nails. Everything looks improvised but sturdy — built from scavenged materials by tiny determined paws. Rope lashings and leaf-thatch details. Coalition includes shrews, voles, and hedgehogs alongside mice.
