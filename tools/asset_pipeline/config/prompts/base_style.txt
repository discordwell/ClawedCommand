Game sprite for a 2D isometric RTS. This must work as an in-game unit at small display sizes.

Art style: Clean digital illustration — Into the Breach's geometric clarity meets Redwall's animal charm. Bold dark outlines (2-3px), flat-filled colors with 2-3 value steps per hue, top-left lighting. High contrast between subject and transparent background. NO background elements, NO ground shadows, NO environmental effects — just the character/unit on a fully transparent PNG.

Critical requirements:
- Readable silhouette at 64px display size
- Subject fills 70-85% of the canvas (no tiny character lost in empty space)
- Bold outlines separate all major shapes
- Colors must be saturated enough to read on any map terrain
- Isometric 3/4 top-down perspective (~30 degrees)
